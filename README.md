# My-Dictionary

## Introduction

Welcome to **My-Dictionary**: your sleek and powerful dictionary app built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With My-Dictionary, you can effortlessly explore word meanings, synonyms, antonyms, and usage examples. Enhance your pronunciation skills with integrated audio, and discover related words through intuitive suggestions.

## Live Demo

Check out the live demo of My-Dictionary [here](https://thebishalprasad.github.io/My-Dictionary/).

## Features

<ul>
  <li><strong>Word Meanings:</strong> Get clear and concise definitions for any word.</li>
  <li><strong>Synonyms & Antonyms:</strong> Find synonyms and antonyms to expand your vocabulary.</li>
  <li><strong>Usage Examples:</strong> See how words are used in sentences.</li>
  <li><strong>Pronunciation Guide:</strong> Improve your pronunciation with integrated audio support.</li>
  <li><strong>Related Words:</strong> Discover related words through intuitive suggestions.</li>
  <li><strong>Bookmark Favorites:</strong> Bookmark your favorite words for quick recall.</li>
  <li><strong>Word of the Day:</strong> Enrich your vocabulary daily with our "Word of the Day" feature.</li>
  <li><strong>User Preferences:</strong> Customize the app to suit your preferences.</li>
</ul>
